# Run and deploy your AI Studio app

This contains everything you need to run your app locally.

View your app in AI Studio: https://ai.studio/apps/92a9f89d-44db-4394-ac9e-0bcd1a7848e9

## Run Locally

**Prerequisites:**  Node.js


1. Install dependencies:
   `npm install`
2. Set the `GEMINI_API_KEY` in [.env.local](.env.local) to your Gemini API key
3. Run the app:
   `npm run dev`

## 📦 Available Modules

Our comprehensive school management platform is built with a modular architecture, allowing schools to enable features as they grow.

### 🚀 Core Academic Management
- **Student Management**: Professional student records from admission to graduation.
- **Class & Section Management**: Effortlessly organize academic levels and sections.
- **Subject Management**: Comprehensive curriculum and teacher-subject assignment.
- **Timetable Builder**: Manage complex schedules with ease.
- **Attendance Tracking**: Real-time daily tracking for both students and staff.
- **Alumni Management**: Transition graduates automatically and maintain engagement.
- **ID Card Generation**: Instant design and printing of student ID cards.

### 💰 Financial & Billing System
- **Fees Management**: Automated invoicing, class-wise fee generation, and receipting.
- **Daily Collections**: Live dashboard for monitoring school revenue.
- **Inventory Sales**: Integrated shop for uniforms, books, and stationery.
- **Scholarship Management**: Transparent application and allocation system.
- **Expenses & Budgeting**: Track departmental spending and financial health.

### 📝 Examination & Assessment
- **Exam Scheduling**: Holistic planning for mid-terms and finals.
- **Grading Scales**: Fully customizable GPA and grading frameworks.
- **Report Card Builder**: Dynamic, professional report templates for any curriculum.
- **CBT Exams**: Computer-Based Testing module for digital assessments.
- **Transcripts & Analysis**: Historical academic records and trend analysis.

### 👥 Human Resources & Payroll
- **Staff Management**: Centralized records for teaching and non-teaching staff.
- **Payroll Processing**: Industrial-grade salary generation and tax management.
- **Performance Insights**: Data-driven appraisals and teacher quality monitoring.
- **Leave Management**: Seamless digital requests and approval tracking.
- **Recruitment Pipeline**: Built-in applicant tracking for school hiring.

### ⚙️ Specialized Operations
- **Library System**: Digital cataloging, borrow/return workflows, and e-books.
- **Transport & Logistics**: Route management, vehicle tracking, and driver logs.
- **Hostel Management**: Manage dormitory allocation and student stays.
- **Health & Medical**: Electronic health records and school clinic management.
- **Inventory & Assets**: Real-time tracking of school property and supplies.

### 🤖 Advanced Smart Features
- **AI Assistant**: Predictive insights for student performance and administrative efficiency.
- **E-Learning Portal**: Integrated online classes, assignments, and study materials.
- **Cloud Storage (Drive)**: Unified file management for staff and students.
